Explaination of files:
 - bank_transactions.csv: A bank customer segmentation dataset offered by Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/bank-customer-segmentation);
 - Location.xlsx: A geographic coordinate information dataset of India (https://www.tageo.com/index-e-in-cities-IN.htm)
 - clus_data.xlsx: The data jointed from above two data, using as the bank customer segmentation example in the paper.
